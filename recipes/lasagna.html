Lasagna

A quick and easy take on a classic dish!

Lasagna can be incredibly intimidating to make, but it doesn't have to be! follow these steps to make an amazing, but quick version of this staple dish

Try it and let me know what you think!

Ingredients:

  • Sheet pasta (I recommend homemade)
  • Marinara sauce
  • Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. layer sauce and pasta
  2. bake at 300 for half an hour
  3. Enjoy!
