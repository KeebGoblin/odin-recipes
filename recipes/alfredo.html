Pasta Alfredo

My favorite recipe for a delicious alfredo sauce

An incredibly rich and creamy dish, I have fond memories of eating this for dinner after long school days. This recipe is taken from my grandmother's cookbook. I hope you all enjoy it.

Try it and let me know what you think!

Ingredients:

  • A pasta of your choice (I will be using fettuccine)
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt, Pepper, and Paprika

Steps:

  1. Put everything in a pan
  2. Call it a day
  3. Enjoy some delicious pasta
