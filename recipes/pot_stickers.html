Pot Stickers

A cheap and easy way to recreate this famous dish at home!

By the end of this recipe, you'll be able to make restaurant-quality pot stickers

Ingredients:

  • Gyoza Wraps
  • Chicken or Vegetable Fillings
  • Salt, Pepper, and Paprika

Steps:

  1. Put everything in a pan
  2. let it fry
  3. Enjoy some delicious pot stickers :)
